Forensics Services

Computer Forensics Services and Forensic Data Recovery

Our forensic analysts collect, preserve, and analyze evidence during an investigation. Forensically there are always tracks or traces left behind, it's just a matter of time before it's discovered. In the eyes of our skilled computer forensics experts, deleted does not always mean un-recoverable, hidden or deleted items become retrievable. Obfuscation is not always obscure.

We can investigate any digital device, and collect evidence of all types of data from a variety of devices and ecosystems. Providing full forensic image preservation for those requiring the collection of files as well as utilise the data collected for legal compliance for our clients from private firms, academia, or government organizations.

In addition to our laboratory role, our forensic scientists can testify as expert witnesses in both criminal and civil cases and can work for either the prosecution or the defense.

Once evidence is collected correctly, it is far more useful in apprehending cyber criminals, and stands a much greater chance of being admissible in the event of a prosecution. Using the latest forensic tools and techniques, our team is able to conduct an examination of provided data or evidence and give a comprehensive report of the findings.
